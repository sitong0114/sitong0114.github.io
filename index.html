Presentation Group for PhD Applicants:

English Practice & Research Discussions

Welcome to our weekly group designed to support PhD applicants in refining their English skills and presentation techniques through research discussions. Each session is a great opportunity to practice presenting academic work in a supportive setting, gain feedback, and engage in discussions on various research topics.

Presentation Guidelines

Meeting Structure & Duration:

  • We meet weekly for about an hour.
  • Each session will feature presentations by three members, each sharing insights on a paper of their choice.
  • After each presentation, another club member will provide a 3-minute evaluation of the speaker's presentation.

Presentation Format:

  • Modeled after an NBER-style discussion:
  • Should include:
    • (i) Motivation behind the paper
    • (ii) Model used
    • (iii) Empirical approach (if applicable)
    • (iv) Results
    • (v) Potential extensions and further research ideas
  • 15 minutes per presentation.
  • Assume all attendees have read the paper, so focus on analysis and insights.
  • Begin with a concise summary in the first one or two slides.
