Introducing TabPy
Predictive Power and Real-Time Insights, All on the Go with TabPy

TabPy: Where Tableau Meets Python, and Innovation Meets Insights.

TabPy is a powerful tool that enables Tableau to run Python code, unlocking advanced capabilities directly within your dashboards. With TabPy, you can embed Python scripts into Tableau calculated fields or extend functionality by adding custom Python functions to the TabPy server. This integration allows your dashboards to become more interactive and dynamic, as Python scripts execute automatically whenever parameters are updated.

While Tableau excels at visualizing data, it can have limitations with advanced analytics like machine learning or complex calculations. By integrating TabPy, you can leverage Python’s rich ecosystem of libraries to implement smarter analytics and display real-time results in your visualizations. This combination empowers you to create dashboards that go beyond static insights, driving more informed decisions.

Health Care Prediction

Imagine you are a doctor wanting to provide your patient with an estimate of their potential risk of heart disease. Instead of explaining probabilities manually, you could simply share an interactive dashboard. The patient can input their data—such as age (e.g., 70 years), tobacco usage (e.g., 4 units), and alcohol consumption (e.g., 12 units). Once they complete the inputs, the model instantly calculates and displays their heart disease risk. With TabPy, you can deliver real-time analysis results on the go!

Loan Default Prediction

Imagine you are a banker, reporting to your boss about why a particular loan application should be declined. Here's how you could streamline the process: Simply open the dashboard and input the client's data, such as age, income, and other relevant details, Your boss can instantly see the analysis and understand the reasoning behind the recommendation. With TabPy, your work becomes highly convenient, analytical, and interpretable. TabPy enables you to view the analysis results in real-time, making decision-making faster and more efficient!

Travel with Tabpy

Imagine you're a visitor to Amsterdam and don't know where to go. This dashboard can help you easily plan your trip using an advanced clustering algorithm (RUNGSS) to analyze and categorize local sights. The analytics divide the attractions into four categories If you’re interested in a place marked in blue, simply click on the point to access its location. You can view the details on Google Maps or directly on the dashboard. The entire service is mobile-friendly, allowing you to plan your trip on the go All analytics run seamlessly using TabPy, making it a powerful and efficient travel companion.
